Postwind is a cyberpunk roleplaying engine that will be used for the game PostWind, also developed by TimeChrome Studios. This engine is coded in C++ by lacaulac, Sventar, Kino, LittlesMonsters and Ercry, five coders of TimeChrome Studio. Happy hacking and playing!
